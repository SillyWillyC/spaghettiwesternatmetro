Synopsis

You and your audiences will eat up this melodrama! With all the classic ingredients — punny names, a meddling villain, and a charming MC who coaches the crowd to boo and cheer — this family-friendly comedy is tailor-made for dinner theatre.

Grannie Smith and her clumsy-but-adorable granddaughter, Petunia Pearl, run the Hoot 'n' Holler Hotel. They're behind on the mortgage, and even Sheriff Kirk Ketchum and his self-appointed deputy, Hal Holdem, can't stop dastardly Duke Dreadful from swooping in to buy the place. World traveler Bonnie Voyage returns from Italy with a plan: a spaghetti sauce contest rich enough to pay the mortgage. Grannie and Petunia enter — but Duke, with accomplice Saucy Sally, will stop at nothing to cheat his way to victory and win Petunia's hand.

Can Sheriff Ketchum save the Hoot 'n' Holler…and win his true love? Let's hope it's not "Mission Im-Pasta-ble!"

  • Audience participation led by an MC: boo the villain, cheer the hero
  • Dinner theatre friendly with flexible, accessible staging
  • High-stakes spaghetti sauce contest drives fast, funny scenes

FAQ

How long is the play?

The show lasts around 1 hour. There will be an intermission near the middle of the play, so you'll have time to get up, use the restroom, or any other things you might need to do.

Is this show suitable for children?

Absolutely! Spaghetti Western is a fun melodrama suitable for all ages to enjoy.

Is there audience participation in the play?

Yes! Our MC (played by the wonderful Hudson Haner) will be assisting the audience in knowing when to boo, cheer, or interact with the characters in the play.

Is there an intermission?

Yes, there will be an intermission! During this time, we will have both a female and male barbershop quartet singing some amazing songs!

Where is the play occuring?

Spaghetti Western will be taking place at the Sewell Mill Library and Cultural Center in Marietta, GA. This is the address:
2051 Lower Roswell Rd, Marietta, GA 30068
The play will be taking place in the Blackbox Theatre within the building.
